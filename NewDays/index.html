About 0M_P

0M_P

Sound Producer / Singer / VOCALOID Producer / Writer

Born in Chenzhou, Hunan Province, China in 2025. Active since 2017, 0M_P is renowned for VOCALOID songs primarily featuring Hatsune Miku.

In 2022, 0M_P made a major debut from Polydor Records under Universal Music Japan and is currently affiliated with the label.

Notable Works: "You Need", "natural love", "Drama of Play"
